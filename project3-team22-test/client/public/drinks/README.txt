Place your drink images in this folder.

Naming convention: lowercase item name with spaces replaced by hyphens + .jpg
Examples:
  Brown Sugar Milk Tea  →  brown-sugar-milk-tea.jpg
  Taro Milk Tea         →  taro-milk-tea.jpg
  Mango Fruit Tea       →  mango-fruit-tea.jpg

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
(if using .png or .webp, update getDrinkImage() in CustomerApp.jsx to match)

Any item without a matching image will automatically fall back to its category emoji.
